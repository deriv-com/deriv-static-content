Neteller has limited its services in country

Neteller has announced that it has stopped offering withdrawals via bank wire in country. This means that you will no longer be able to make withdrawals via bank wire using Neteller in the Deriv cashier.

Your account and funds on Deriv are safe and unaffected by this.

If you’ve only been using Neteller to fund your Deriv account, here’s how to enable a new payment method for both deposits and withdrawals.

Here’s how to enable a new payment method for both deposits and withdrawals.

Enable a new payment method

1. Log in to your account and select your real money account. Then, go to Cashier and click on Deposit.

For Deriv:

2. Choose a new payment method from the list and make a minimum deposit into your Deriv account.

Please note that withdrawals via cards are restricted in some countries; you may need to use an e-wallet service.

3. If the deposit is successful, you’ll be able to see that method in the withdrawal screen and make withdrawals using the new payment method from here on.

Please let us know if you’re having trouble adding a new payment method; we’re here to help.
