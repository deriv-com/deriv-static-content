You can ramp up your marketing efforts and get more clicks on your ads. Here are 4 easy and useful tips to help improve your marketing strategy to get more clients and earn more commission.

Use SEO keywords

Using search engine optimisation (SEO) keywords makes it easier for potential clients to find you. To get you started with keyword research, watch our training video on how to find the right keywords using the Google Keyword Planner.
Watch our SEO training video

Create an ad copy that stands out

When writing your ad copy, think about your clients' goals and address them in your copy. Your ads should present a solution to their query or problem. Be as creative as you can to get your information out in an interesting way, and incorporate your keywords and a call to action.

Target the right audience

Your target audience refers to the group of people to whom you want to sell your products or services to. To clearly define who your target audience is, identify your current customer demographics — age, location, gender, educational background, social class, interests, and search behaviour. Factor in the time you should show your ads and the device used to view the ads, so that your prospects have the best user experience.

Build an online community

Online communities can help create meaningful connections between you and your customers. By having an online community such as a Facebook group, you have the opportunity to interact with them and learn about their interests.
