In focus this week

Training videos for partners

The objective of this project is to offer training for our partners on the best practices to promote Deriv. We have concluded 2 phases up until now.
  • Phase 1 featured a number of videos teaching partners on how to promote Deriv using different digital marketing tools and social platforms.
  • Phase 2, concluded last week, taught our partners the best practices in promoting Deriv in different regions.

We're now taking a break for 2 weeks as we gear up for Phase 3, where we will teach our partners all they need to know about our products and platforms to successfully promote them. In this gap-time, we'll prepare the scripts for Phase 3 as well as run a survey for partners, to see what else they'd like to learn more about. We aim to conclude phase 3 and consider this project done and dusted by early Q3.

Curious to know what we're talking about? Check out our partnerships playlists on YouTube for Phase 1 and Phase 2 .

Deriv X launch

We are getting ready to launch Deriv X in the markets. We're working on a number of marketing materials - the emails have been designed and have moved on to front-end and we're just giving a finishing touch to some nice social media posts and banners for the blog.
