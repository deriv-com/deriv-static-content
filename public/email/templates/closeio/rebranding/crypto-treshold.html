We're raising the minimum threshold for our crypto commission payouts

Hi {{ lead.display_name }},

We've increased the minimum threshold for our cryptocurrency payments from 200 USD to 500 USD. This change will affect your next commission payout onwards.

This means that if you have any commission below the new threshold, you won't be able to receive it until your total commission reaches 500 USD. 

If your total commission is below the minimum threshold in the next cycle, you'll need to change your payment method to your Deriv account or Neteller account to receive it within the same cycle.
