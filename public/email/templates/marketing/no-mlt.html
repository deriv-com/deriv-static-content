Due to upcoming changes to our licence, we will stop offering the MLT account in the UK from 29 Sep 2021 onwards. You can continue to trade on Deriv, just with different accounts.

What does this mean for me?

This means that your MLT account will be closed on 29 Sep 2021 at 12:00 pm (GMT), but you can continue to trade CFDs and multipliers with a Financial account.

What should I do?

1. Close all positions in your MLT account

Check your open positions in the Reports screen and close them before 29 Sep 2021, 12:00 pm (GMT).

2. Move your funds out of your MLT account

You can either

• Withdraw your funds, or

• Transfer your funds from your MLT account to your Financial account. If you don’t have a Financial account, see how to add one below.

How do I add a Financial account?

Step 1:
Log in to your Deriv account.

Step 2:
Open the Balance drop-down menu on the top right side of your screen and click on Add a Financial account.

How can I continue trading digital options?

You’ll need to close your MLT account and sign up for a new Deriv account. You’ll get a new MX account, which will give you access to digital options on synthetics.

Note: The MX account will only be available for a few more months.
