As part of the changes in our product line-up, we will be closing Options accounts belonging to our clients in Europe.

What this means for you

From 12 November 2021 onwards, you won’t be able to trade digital options on any of our platforms. Also, you won’t be able to make deposits into your Options account. You need to withdraw any funds from your Options account as soon as possible.

What you need to do now

1. Close all your digital options positions before 12 November 2021, 12:00 am GMT

  • If you’re using Deriv — you can ﬁnd your open positions in Reports.

  • If you’re using Binary.com — you can ﬁnd your open positions in the Portfolio.

If you still have open positions on digital options on 12 November 2021, 12:00 am GMT, we shall close them and refund you.

2. Withdraw your funds from your Options account

You will lose access to your Options account when it gets closed, so be sure to withdraw all your funds. (If you have a Deriv CFDs account, you can also transfer the funds from your Options account to your CFDs account.)
