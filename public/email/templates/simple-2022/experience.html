Checklist for a secure trading experience

Hi [client name],

You deserve a safe and secure Deriv P2P experience. Here are some useful tips to keep your information and transactions safe online:

Keep your P2P conversations in-app

The chat feature in Deriv P2P is the best place to confirm payment methods, share receipts, and other transaction details with the trader you’re dealing with. All your P2P-related conversations are logged and can be used to resolve any disputes that may arise.

Deal with reliable traders

Look for traders with good track records/stats on Deriv P2P. Completion rates, the average time taken to pay, and trade volumes are good indicators of reliability. Another important tip: Always, always ensure the trader you’re dealing with is ID- and address-verified.

Keep your information private

We’ll never ask for your account details via email or social media. So be careful when someone unknown to you asks for your information.

Get paid first

If you’re selling on Deriv P2P, always confirm that you’ve received payment from the buyer before releasing funds to them. Don’t complete any transaction until you get paid first.

Fortify your online security

Use multi-factor authentication and ensure that your device has the latest security solutions (antivirus, firewalls, etc). Update your apps and software and always back up your data.
