Be careful. Don’t get scammed.

As scammers are getting more resourceful, we just wanted to share some tips on protecting your information and staying safe online:

Do you know the sender?

Always check the details at the top of the email message, and make sure you recognise the sender’s info before responding. Don’t respond to an email if you don’t recognise the sender’s name or email address.

Look closely

Sometimes, scammers use email addresses that are similar to addresses that you trust. For example, instead of support@deriv.com, you might see something like support@deriv1.com or support-deriv@gmail.com. See the difference?

When in doubt, check with us

If you receive a suspicious email asking to upload documents or reset a password, and if you need further clarification, don’t hesitate to contact us. We’ll confirm if we need anything from you.
