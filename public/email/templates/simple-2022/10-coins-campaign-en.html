Check out 10Coins on Deriv, a reliable crypto-to-fiat service that allows you to seamlessly deposit USD into your Deriv account, so that you never miss a trade.

All you need is your cryptocurrency and a crypto wallet (10Coins supports a variety of digital coins, so you don't have to worry about the type of coin you have). What's more, you don't need a 10Coins account and it's completely free to use!

Make a deposit via your crypto wallet in just 7 steps:

Step 1
Go to Cashier in your Deriv account, select Deposit via bank wire, credit card, and e-wallet button.

Step 2
Select 10Coins.

Step 3
Enter the amount in USD that you want to deposit and click Next.

Step 4
Click Continue and you will be directed to a new tab.

Step 5
From the drop-down menu, select the wallet currency you want to proceed with.

Step 6
Select the cryptocurrency of your choice and click Submit.

Note: The service also offers the stablecoin USDT (ERC 20 and TRC20).

Step 7
Scan the QR code or copy and paste the token address into your crypto wallet to complete the payment. Once the payment is complete, click the Payment Sent button.

Your Deriv account will be credited with USD in an hour's time.
