What we did last year

These were some of the biggest events of 2022.

Africa Affiliate Conference, Rwanda

Gathering our top African affiliates, we had our first ever regional Affiliate Conference in Kigali, Rwanda, from 30 September to 2 October 2022. The conference provided us with the opportunity to meet our affiliates and for them to network with one another. We also held a gala night, giving special recognition to the most outstanding affiliates in Africa.

Jordan Financial Expo

We were awarded the Best Affiliate Programme award at the Jordan Financial Expo (JFEX) which took place early last November in Amman, Jordan. By receiving this award, we’re moving towards being a recognised and trusted brand in the MENA region.

Portuguese Conference, Paraguay

On 30 November and 1 December 2022, we had a conference in Asunción, Paraguay, specially for our top 40 affiliates in Brazil. This group consisted of affiliates that happen to be among the best within our network. Like the Africa conference, we held a gala night to honour our top affiliates for their contribution.

What we did this quarter

Here are the highlights of the past few months.

South Asian Affiliate Conference, Dubai

On 24 to 27 February 2023, we caught up with our affiliates from the South Asian region. Top affiliates from India, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, and Nepal were brought together to Dubai for the conference, which also ended in a dinner appreciating them.

Affiliate World Conference, Dubai

We also participated in the Affiliate World Conference in Dubai in March, where our people met with existing and potential partners. This conference was the largest gathering of the world’s top affiliate marketers and e-commerce entrepreneurs.
