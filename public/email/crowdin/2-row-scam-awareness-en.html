1. Verify the account

2. Check the account activity
Misspelling of the account name, a high following count, and emoji-loaded offers that sound too good to be true are red flags.

We never ask for personal or bank details through social media, or do giveaways or promotions. Only trust info posted on our website, official social media accounts and emails ending in @deriv.com.

3. Report the profile
If you come across a fake profile, use the report button on the platform to flag the account as fraudulent. This process will take less than a minute and helps the platform take appropriate action.

For Facebook, Instagram and Twitter, tap on the 3 dots on the profile and click Report.

4. Contact Customer Support
After reporting the fake profile, please provide screenshots of their account to our Customer Support team via live chat so we can take additional measures.
