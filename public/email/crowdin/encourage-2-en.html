10 days to open a real Deriv account

Hi [Affiliate’s name],

To continue receiving commissions, please open a real Deriv account and verify your account within 10 days.

This will ensure you receive your commissions promptly and without hiccups.

If you fail to open a real Deriv account within this period, your affiliate account will be suspended, and you may not receive your commissions.

Here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Open your real Deriv account

To get started, you'll need to create a free Deriv demo account. If you already have a demo account, log in to that account and continue to create a real Deriv account.

Once you’ve created or logged in to your Deriv demo account, go to Trader's Hub, click the Demo drop-down, then select Real.

Click the Get button and Choose your account currency and complete all the required information to open a real Deriv account.

Step 2: Verify your account

After setting up your Deriv real account, we need you to verify your account by submitting your proof of identity and proof of address.

To verify your identity, upload a photo of any of the following documents:
Passport Driver’s licence Identity card

To verify your address, upload a photo of your recent bank statement or utility bill (no more than 12 months old) that clearly shows your name and current address.

Examples of utility bills that we accept:
Electricity Water Gas

Once your account is verified, your commissions will be credited to your real trading account, and you can withdraw from your account using your preferred payment method.

If you have any questions, please reach out to us via live chat or contact your Country manager.
