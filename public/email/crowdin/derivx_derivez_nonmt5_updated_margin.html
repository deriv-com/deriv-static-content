We’re updating our margin requirements

Starting 18 Sep 2023 at 12:00 GMT, fully-hedged positions on all Deriv CFD trading platforms will require margin.

The following example shows how the updated margin requirements will work on your positions:

Actions (in chronological order) Margin requirement before the change Margin requirement after the change
Open long 0.01 lots of Volatility 75 Index at 100,000 USD 1 USD 1 USD
Open short 0.01 lots of Volatility 75 Index at 100,000 USD 0 USD 1 USD

Please make sure you have sufficient funds in all your CFD trading accounts to keep your positions open. If you don’t have enough funds, your trades may be stopped out.
