Closing Deriv accounts in specific regions

হাই {{ Profile.firstName | default:"trader" }},

We're writing to inform you about upcoming changes to Deriv's operational territories. Effective immediately, Deriv will no longer offer services to clients in the following regions:

  • South Georgia and South Sandwich Islands

  • French Southern Territories

  • Heard Island and McDonald Islands

  • Bouvet Island

  • St. Vincent and the Grenadines

  • Mauritius

What this means for you

  • From 30 Oct 2024 onwards, you won’t be able to trade on any Deriv platforms.

  • You can still log in to your Deriv account and access your Deriv MT5, Deriv cTrader, and Deriv X accounts, but you won’t be able to make deposits.

  • Your accounts will be closed on 05 Nov, and you won’t be able to access Cashier.

What you need to do

  • Close any open trades
    Make sure to close any open trades within the next 72 hours.

  • Withdraw your funds
    Withdraw your remaining funds before 05 Nov 2024, as you won’t be able to access them after your account is closed. Due to processing restrictions, we are unable to process withdrawals for balances under USD 5.

We encourage you to withdraw any amount above USD 5 to avoid losing access to your funds.
