As part of our commitment to your safety on Deriv, we want to share essential tips to help you identify genuine advertisers and avoid scams.
An advertiser may not be genuine if they
  • Offer ad prices that seem “too good to be true”.
  • Have a newly created profile with no trading activity.
  • Ask you to complete transactions outside of Deriv P2P.
  • Pressure you to release funds before confirming payment.
  • Claim payment is made, but no deposit appears in your bank account.
  • Send receipts with mismatched details, spelling errors, or unusual formatting.
  • Delay payments.
A genuine advertiser usually has
  • A high completion rate and successful trade history.
  • Positive ratings and user reviews.
  • Verified payment methods.
  • Verified identity and contact information.
  • Fair pricing aligned with market rates.
For secure transactions, always
  • Keep all communication and transactions within Deriv P2P.
  • Verify payment before releasing funds.
  • Trade only with trusted and verified advertisers.
  • Avoid sharing personal banking details outside Deriv P2P transactions.

Deriv will never ask for passwords, verification codes, or sensitive financial information. If you notice anything suspicious, report it immediately via live chat and WhatsApp. For more details, check out our safety guidelines.
