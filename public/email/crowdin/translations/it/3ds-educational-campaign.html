If your card deposit didn’t work recently, your bank may have blocked it because the 3D Secure (3DS) check wasn’t completed.

3DS is a security step used by your bank to prevent fraudulent activity. When you make a payment online, they will ask you to confirm it, usually by sending a message or notification to your phone.

Here’s how to make sure your next
deposit goes through:

1. From Deriv Cashier, choose to deposit by card.

2. Enter your card details, and click Deposit now.

3. You’ll get a message or notification from your bank asking you to confirm the payment.

4. Follow the steps from your bank. This may include entering a code, using your banking app, or replying to a message.

5. Once confirmed, your deposit will be processed.

Next time you make a deposit, check your phone right away for a message or notification from your bank. If nothing comes through, 3DS might not be enabled for your card. Without it, your bank can’t confirm the payment, and the transaction may be declined.

To resolve this, ask your bank how to activate and use 3DS for online payments. They’ll guide you through the process.
